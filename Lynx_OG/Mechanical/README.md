# Lynx OG - Mechanical Specifications

## Frame
* **Material:** Magnesium alloy — lightweight, corrosion resistant, good thermal dissipation.
* **Design:** Two-piece shell with internal structural frame.

## Suspension
* **Type:** FastAce hydraulic dual-shock suspension.
* **Travel:** 80mm / 90mm (depending on configuration).
* **Adjustments:** Rebound, compression, and preload.
* **Spring Weights:**
  - 62 lbs — riders up to 190 lbs
  - 66 lbs — riders 190-220 lbs
  - 70 lbs — riders over 220 lbs

## Wheel & Tire
* **Tire Size:** 20" | 80/90-14 (tubeless).
* **Tire Options:** Knobby (off-road) or Street (pavement).
* **Rim:** Motor hub-integrated rim.

## Motor
* **Type:** Hall-less brushless DC hub motor.
* **Nominal Power:** 3,200W.
* **Peak Power:** 8,000W (8KW).
* **Phase Wires:** G (Green), B (Blue), Y (Yellow) — screw terminal connection to controller.
* **Motor Mounting:** Axle-through design with suspension linkage.

## Pedals
* **Type:** Folding pedals, fixed height.
* **Attachment:** Bracket-mounted to the shell body.
* **Grip Surface:** Molded studs (not replaceable on OG — Lynx-S upgraded to replaceable studs).

## Kickstand
* **Type:** Fixed (non-folding) kickstand.
* **Material:** Steel/aluminum.

## Trolley Handle
* **Type:** Retractable.
